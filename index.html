About the event

Three days where Dutch precision meets Indian cultivation.

India Mushroom Days brings the world's most advanced growing science to the fastest-growing market on earth. We exist to close the gap between what Dutch greenhouses have perfected and what Indian farms are ready to scale.

/ MISSION

Grow smarter, together

Equip every grower — from single-tunnel farms to industrial substrate plants — with climate control, robotics and yield science that actually works in Indian conditions.

/ VISION

One million tonnes by 2032

A connected national network of high-efficiency mushroom producers, supported by research, supply chain and export pathways.

/ ORGANIZERS

IMD Council + KAS partners

Convened by the India Mushroom Council alongside Dutch agritech institutes, with backing from state agriculture boards.
